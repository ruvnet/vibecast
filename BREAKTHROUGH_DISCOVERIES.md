### 8. Accident Tolerant Fuels (ATF) ⚙️

**Novelty: 9.0/10 | Impact: Very High | Timeframe: 5-7 years**

**The Upgrade:**
Next-generation fuel with chromium-coated zirconium or SiC cladding. Withstands 1400°C+ (vs 1200°C for standard Zircaloy).

**Impact:**
- 10-15% fuel cost reduction
- Enhanced safety margins (critical post-Fukushima)
- Extended fuel cycles (18 → 24 months)
- Reduced refueling outages

**Implementation Path:**
- Lead test assemblies (LTAs) starting Cycle N
- Real-time fuel performance monitoring
- Gradual core conversion (20% per cycle)
- Full core conversion in 5-6 cycles

**Regulatory:** NRC 10 CFR 50.46a requires demonstration. Several vendors have active programs (Framatome, Westinghouse, GE).

**ROI:** $20-30M/year savings when fully implemented
