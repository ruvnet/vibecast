# Scope Definition: Novel Cancer Curing Algorithm Research

**Objective:** To research and identify potential novel algorithmic approaches for cancer treatment based on the most current, cutting-edge scientific research available through Perplexity AI.

**Scope:**
- Focus on algorithmic or computational approaches, potentially involving AI, machine learning, bioinformatics, systems biology, or computational modeling.
- Explore recent breakthroughs (last 1-3 years) in cancer research, including genomics, proteomics, immunology, targeted therapies, and drug discovery.
- Identify algorithms that aim to personalize treatment, predict treatment response, discover new therapeutic targets, or optimize existing therapies.
- Exclude purely biological or chemical approaches unless they are directly driven or enabled by a novel computational algorithm.
- The output will be a structured research report detailing findings, analysis, and potential directions.

**Initial Boundaries:**
- Primary information source: Perplexity AI searches.
- Timeframe for research focus: Primarily 2022-Present.
- Exclude clinical trial results unless they specifically validate an algorithmic approach.
