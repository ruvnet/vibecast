# AISP Human Guide — Understanding AI-First Specifications

> **Status:** Placeholder — Tutorials coming soon

AISP is designed for AI agents as the primary audience, but humans need to write and review specifications too. This guide bridges that gap.

**Quick start for humans:**
1. Read [AI_GUIDE.md](./AI_GUIDE.md) alongside your AI
2. Ask your AI to explain each block (`⟦Ω⟧`, `⟦Σ⟧`, etc.)
3. Practice converting natural language requirements to AISP with AI assistance
